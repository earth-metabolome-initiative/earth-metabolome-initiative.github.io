Susan is a plant evolutionary ecologist and chemical ecologist.

## Collaboration statement

I am interested in contributing to the Earth Metabolome Initiative by sharing expertise in plant chemical ecology, frugivory and seed dispersal, and phytochemical diversity.  I hope to advance field data collection efforts in temperate and tropical forests and the incorporation of intraspecific and intraplant metabolomic variation into the project.
